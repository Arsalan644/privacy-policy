Privacy Policy — GENWALLS

Last updated: December 16, 2025

Overview

GENWALLS values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, username, and account details to sign up, log in, and personalize your experience.
  • Device & usage data: device type, operating system, language preferences, and app usage metrics for performance, stability and analytics.
  • Prompts & generated content: prompts you submit and generated wallpapers may be temporarily stored for processing and to improve the service. These are not shared with third parties for advertising or resale.

Use of Information

We use information to provide, operate and improve GENWALLS, to personalize your experience, respond to support requests, and to analyze performance. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data.

Sharing & Third Parties

We may share data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, processors) who are contractually bound to protect your information. We will disclose information when required by law or to protect legal rights.

Retention & Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or to comply with legal obligations, after which we securely delete it. You may request deletion of your account and related data by emailing support@genwalls.app. We will verify and process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

GENWALLS is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.

Consent & Updates

By using GENWALLS you consent to the collection and use of information described in this policy. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; the latest version will be available in the app. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy can be submitted to support@genwalls.app.
